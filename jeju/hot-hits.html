Black Pork Burgers · 흑돼지 버거

The SNS food trend that defines 2025 Jeju

Somewhere between a meme and a movement: Jeju's black pork reimagined as a burger patty. These went viral on Korean Instagram and are now a mandatory photo stop.

🍔
Viral

제주 몬트락 버거Jeju Montlac Burger

₩8,000–12,000
Black Pork Patty Burger · 흑돼지 버거

The original SNS-viral black pork burger. A patty made from actual Jeju 흑돼지, grilled and assembled with the kind of care that turns a burger into content. The photos from here circulate Korean food Instagram like currency.

Why it went viral: It's not a gimmick — the black pork actually tastes different. The fat-to-lean ratio is closer to wagyu than a standard beef patty, and the Jeju-sourcing story gives it authenticity. Go before noon to avoid the longest queues.
제주시 · Jeju City area Search Naver for current pop-up locations
🍔
Viral

블랙버거하우스Black Burger House

₩8,000–12,000
Black Pork Patty Burger · 흑돼지 버거

The other viral black pork burger — the rival to Montlac. SNS-popular for the same reasons: real Jeju black pork, photogenic presentation, and a patty that justifies the trend rather than riding it.

제주시 · Jeju City area Instagram-famous · trending 2025

Desserts & Sweets · 디저트

Tangerine castella, matcha-hallabong gelato, and Udo peanut ice cream
🍦
Iconic

오설록 티뮤지엄Osulloc Tea Museum

₩5,000–8,000
Matcha & Hallabong Gelato · 말차 한라봉 젤라또

The icon. Osulloc grows its own matcha on Jeju and turns it into gelato swirled with 한라봉 (hallabong — the prized Jeju citrus). The museum itself is a pilgrimage site for tea lovers. The gelato is the move — two flavours in one cup, intensely green, intensely orange.

Pro tip: The museum shop sells takeaway tea and desserts. Buy the matcha powder — it's the same stuff they use in the gelato, and you can't get this quality off-island.
제주 특별자치도 서귀포시 안덕면 신화역사로 15 Near Jungmun area · free parking
🍰
Trending

귤향당Gulhyangdang

₩4,000–7,000
Tangerine Cream Castella · 감귤 크림카스텔라

Moist castella cake made with Jeju 감귤 (tangerines). The tangerine cream layer adds a citrusy tang that cuts the sweetness — a distinctly Jeju bake. One of the bakery trends that's blown up on Korean social media in recent seasons.

제주시 · Jeju City area Sells out — arrive early
🍰
Cafe

카페 델문도Cafe Del Mundo

₩4,000–8,000
Tangerine Desserts & Ocean View · 감귤 디저트

Also tangerine cream castella, but in a completely different setting: right in front of the sea with a "cinematic atmosphere" (영화 같은 감성). The combo of citrus dessert and ocean view is peak Jeju Instagram content.

세화/구좌 · Sehwa/Gujwa area, east coast Ocean front · cinematic vibe
🥜
Classic

우도왕자Udo Prince

₩3,000–6,000
Udo Peanut Ice Cream · 우도 땅콩 아이스크림

The ferry-trip classic. Located on Udo Island (a short ferry from the east coast), serving ice cream, cream bread, and lattes made from peanuts grown on Udo itself. The peanuts here have a "richer, deeper flavor than regular peanuts" — the volcanic soil does the work.

The ritual: Take the ferry to Udo, rent a bike, ride the coastal loop, stop here for peanut ice cream. It's the single most photographed food moment on Jeju Instagram, and it genuinely tastes like the island.
우도 · Udo Island (ferry from Seongsan Port) Peanut ice cream, cream bread, latte

Ocean-View Cafes · 감성 카페

The aesthetic cafe trail that rivals the food

Jeju's cafe scene is a destination in itself. These are the ones worth a deliberate detour — sorted by coast so you can route them into your road trip.

☕
Film

봄날Bomnal / Spring Day

₩5,000–8,000
Aewol Ocean-View Cafe · 애월 감성 카페

Aewol's representative ocean-view cafe. Famous as a movie filming location — the yellow bus in the courtyard is the photo zone everyone comes for. The cafe connects naturally to the sea. Their Bomnal Latte and carrot cake are the signatures.

Timing: Go early in the morning for fewer crowds and better light. Afternoon gets packed with tour groups. The Aewol coastal road is beautiful at golden hour — plan the drive accordingly.
애월 해안도로 · Aewol Coastal Road Movie filming location · yellow bus photo zone
🌊
View

리브레 제주Libre Jeju

₩6,000–9,000
Hyeopjae Beach Cafe · 협재 감성 끝판왕

Called the "ultimate Hyeopjae Beach 감성 cafe" (협재 해변 감성 끝판왕). Full-wall glass windows facing the ocean. Vanilla latte, Jeju carrot cake, and Einspänner (Viennese coffee). Go at sunset for the golden sea views.

협재 해변 앞 · Right in front of Hyeopjae Beach Parking available · go at golden hour
📸
Instagram

카페 월정리101Cafe Woljeongri 101

₩5,000–8,000
Woljeongri Beach Cafe · 인스타 핫플

Described as "hot on Instagram" (인스타에서 핫한) for its ocean view. Located in the famous Woljeongri beach area on the east coast — the emerald water and white sand make this the most photogenic beach on the island, and this cafe is positioned to prove it.

세화/구좌 · Woljeongri Beach, east coast Instagram-famous · ocean view
☕
Coffee

카페 테라로사 제주Terrarosa Jeju

₩5,000–8,000
Coffee Roaster · 커피 전문 + 분위기

Known as both a "coffee specialty spot" and a "vibe spot" (커피 맛집 + 분위기 맛집). Seogwipo's top roaster — the rare cafe where the coffee quality matches the architecture. Come for the beans, stay for the space.

서귀포 · Seogwipo area Coffee + atmosphere · roaster on-site
🍵
Tea

서귀다원Seogwi Dawon

₩5,000–8,000
Tea House · 유자차 · 녹차 라떼

A calm, quiet tea house surrounded by green nature views near Geum Oreum. Tea-based menu — yuja cha (citron tea), green tea latte. Operates as a no-kids zone (노키즈존). Perfect for healing between meals. Window seats and outdoor tables are ideal for reading.

제주시~서귀포 중간 · Midpoint between Jeju City & Seogwipo, near 금오름 No-kids zone · nature views

Unique Finds · 특별한 발견

The dishes and spots that don't fit any category — and that's the point
🍜
Unique

한라면옥Hallamyeonok

₩8,000–10,000
Hallasan Noodles · 한라산 국수

A Jeju-only dish: bibim (spicy mixed) noodles topped with a piece of broth ice shaped like Mt. Hallasan. As the ice melts, it transforms the spicy bibim into a complex hybrid broth. Gimmicky? Yes. But it works — the temperature and dilution arc is genuinely interesting.

Also at 명랑식당 (Myeongrang Sikdang): The same dish, slightly different execution. Try both if you're a noodle completist. The "mountain" melting is genuinely photogenic — this is one viral food moment that earns its posts.
제주시 · Jeju City area Also at: 명랑식당
🥟
Authentic

천일만두Cheonil Mandu

₩9,000–25,000
Authentic Chinese Dumplings · 중국식 만두 전문

Authentic mainland Chinese dumplings in the middle of Seogwipo. Run by Chinese owners and staff. The signature 눈꽃만두 (snowflake dumplings, ₩10,000) — fried dumplings coated with starch water, crispy on top and juicy on bottom. The 부추군만두 (chive dumplings with shrimp) has "explosive juice." Don't skip the 마파두부 (mapo tofu, ₩12,000) — authentic Sichuan, numbing and spicy.

Why it's here: This is the kind of place you don't expect on Jeju — a genuinely good Chinese restaurant in a Korean food paradise. Tables fill up fast after break time. ~10 tables, open kitchen, cozy. Near Seogwipo 매일올레시장 (Olle Market).
서귀포시 서문로 25 · Near Olle Market, Seogwipo 10:00–22:00 · Break 14:00–17:00 · Closed Thursdays Phone: 0507-1414-9799 · No private parking — use 공영주차장
🍗
TV

희신이네Heuisinne

₩9,000–15,000
Korean-Chinese Chicken & Chinese Food · 닭요리 + 중식

Featured on YouTube's "또간집" (Tteoggan Jip) hosted by 풍자 (Poongja). A Korean-Chinese chicken and Chinese food specialist. The 닭고기냉밀면 (cold chicken noodle, ~₩9,000) is the blogger's #1 pick — a unique cold milmyeon with rich chicken broth. The 순살 닭깐풍기 (boneless fried chicken kanpunggi) is the other reason to come.

The name: Named after the owner's daughter. "Better when you lower your expectations," wrote one reviewer — then proceeded to eat everything. Open kitchen, long-established local feel. The chicken kanpunggi's sweetness may vary by who cooks it — that's part of the charm.
서귀포시 서홍로 175 · Seogwipo ~11:00–20:30 Also serves: 짬뽕, 해물짬뽕밥, 내장볶음밥
🏪
Market

동문시장Dongmun Market

₩2,000–15,000
Traditional Market Street Food · 시장 먹거리

Jeju's biggest traditional market. The place to eat 해물파전 (seafood & green onion pancake), sample fresh tangerines, find 빙떡 (buckwheat rice cake), buy 자리돔젓 (rabbitfish jeotgal), and graze through street food stalls. The energy is the attraction as much as the food.

Strategy: Come hungry, don't commit to one stall. Walk the lanes, taste as you go, buy tangerine gifts at the end. This is also where you'll find the best 오메기떡 (omegi rice cake) and Jeju-specific condiments to take home.
제주시 · Central Jeju City Open daily · best in morning for fresh produce, evening for food stalls
