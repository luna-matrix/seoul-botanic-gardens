Black Pork Burgers · 흑돼지 버거

The SNS food trend that defines 2025 Jeju

Somewhere between a meme and a movement: Jeju's black pork reimagined as a burger patty. These went viral on Korean Instagram and are now a mandatory photo stop.

🍔
Viral

제주 몬트락 버거Jeju Montlac Burger

₩8,000–12,000
Black Pork Patty Burger · 흑돼지 버거

The original SNS-viral black pork burger. A patty made from actual Jeju 흑돼지, grilled and assembled with the kind of care that turns a burger into content. The photos from here circulate Korean food Instagram like currency.

Why it went viral: It's not a gimmick — the black pork actually tastes different. The fat-to-lean ratio is closer to wagyu than a standard beef patty, and the Jeju-sourcing story gives it authenticity. Go before noon to avoid the longest queues.
제주시 · Jeju City area Search Naver for current pop-up locations
🍔
Viral

블랙버거하우스Black Burger House

₩8,000–12,000
Black Pork Patty Burger · 흑돼지 버거

The other viral black pork burger — the rival to Montlac. SNS-popular for the same reasons: real Jeju black pork, photogenic presentation, and a patty that justifies the trend rather than riding it.

제주시 · Jeju City area Instagram-famous · trending 2025

Desserts & Sweets · 디저트

Tangerine castella, matcha-hallabong gelato, and Udo peanut ice cream
🍦
Iconic

오설록 티뮤지엄Osulloc Tea Museum

₩5,000–8,000
Matcha & Hallabong Gelato · 말차 한라봉 젤라또

The icon. Osulloc grows its own matcha on Jeju and turns it into gelato swirled with 한라봉 (hallabong — the prized Jeju citrus). The museum itself is a pilgrimage site for tea lovers. The gelato is the move — two flavours in one cup, intensely green, intensely orange.

Pro tip: The museum shop sells takeaway tea and desserts. Buy the matcha powder — it's the same stuff they use in the gelato, and you can't get this quality off-island.
제주 특별자치도 서귀포시 안덕면 신화역사로 15 Near Jungmun area · free parking
🍰
Trending

귤향당Gulhyangdang

₩4,000–7,000
Tangerine Cream Castella · 감귤 크림카스텔라

Moist castella cake made with Jeju 감귤 (tangerines). The tangerine cream layer adds a citrusy tang that cuts the sweetness — a distinctly Jeju bake. One of the bakery trends that's blown up on Korean social media in recent seasons.

제주시 · Jeju City area Sells out — arrive early
🍰
Cafe

카페 델문도Cafe Del Mundo

₩4,000–8,000
Tangerine Desserts & Ocean View · 감귤 디저트

Also tangerine cream castella, but in a completely different setting: right in front of the sea with a "cinematic atmosphere" (영화 같은 감성). The combo of citrus dessert and ocean view is peak Jeju Instagram content.

세화/구좌 · Sehwa/Gujwa area, east coast Ocean front · cinematic vibe
🥜
Classic

우도왕자Udo Prince

₩3,000–6,000
Udo Peanut Ice Cream · 우도 땅콩 아이스크림

The ferry-trip classic. Located on Udo Island (a short ferry from the east coast), serving ice cream, cream bread, and lattes made from peanuts grown on Udo itself. The peanuts here have a "richer, deeper flavor than regular peanuts" — the volcanic soil does the work.

The ritual: Take the ferry to Udo, rent a bike, ride the coastal loop, stop here for peanut ice cream. It's the single most photographed food moment on Jeju Instagram, and it genuinely tastes like the island.
우도 · Udo Island (ferry from Seongsan Port) Peanut ice cream, cream bread, latte

Ocean-View Cafes · 감성 카페

The aesthetic cafe trail that rivals the food

Jeju's cafe scene is a destination in itself. These are the ones worth a deliberate detour — sorted by coast so you can route them into your road trip.

☕
Film

봄날Bomnal / Spring Day

₩5,000–8,000
Aewol Ocean-View Cafe · 애월 감성 카페

Aewol's representative ocean-view cafe. Famous as a movie filming location — the yellow bus in the courtyard is the photo zone everyone comes for. The cafe connects naturally to the sea. Their Bomnal Latte and carrot cake are the signatures.

Timing: Go early in the morning for fewer crowds and better light. Afternoon gets packed with tour groups. The Aewol coastal road is beautiful at golden hour — plan the drive accordingly.
애월 해안도로 · Aewol Coastal Road Movie filming location · yellow bus photo zone
🌊
View

리브레 제주Libre Jeju

₩6,000–9,000
Hyeopjae Beach Cafe · 협재 감성 끝판왕

Called the "ultimate Hyeopjae Beach 감성 cafe" (협재 해변 감성 끝판왕). Full-wall glass windows facing the ocean. Vanilla latte, Jeju carrot cake, and Einspänner (Viennese coffee). Go at sunset for the golden sea views.

협재 해변 앞 · Right in front of Hyeopjae Beach Parking available · go at golden hour
📸
Instagram

카페 월정리101Cafe Woljeongri 101

₩5,000–8,000
Woljeongri Beach Cafe · 인스타 핫플

Described as "hot on Instagram" (인스타에서 핫한) for its ocean view. Located in the famous Woljeongri beach area on the east coast — the emerald water and white sand make this the most photogenic beach on the island, and this cafe is positioned to prove it.

세화/구좌 · Woljeongri Beach, east coast Instagram-famous · ocean view
☕
Coffee

카페 테라로사 제주Terrarosa Jeju

₩5,000–8,000
Coffee Roaster · 커피 전문 + 분위기

Known as both a "coffee specialty spot" and a "vibe spot" (커피 맛집 + 분위기 맛집). Seogwipo's top roaster — the rare cafe where the coffee quality matches the architecture. Come for the beans, stay for the space.

서귀포 · Seogwipo area Coffee + atmosphere · roaster on-site
🍵
Tea

서귀다원Seogwi Dawon

₩5,000–8,000
Tea House · 유자차 · 녹차 라떼

A calm, quiet tea house surrounded by green nature views near Geum Oreum. Tea-based menu — yuja cha (citron tea), green tea latte. Operates as a no-kids zone (노키즈존). Perfect for healing between meals. Window seats and outdoor tables are ideal for reading.

제주시~서귀포 중간 · Midpoint between Jeju City & Seogwipo, near 금오름 No-kids zone · nature views

Bakeries, Bagels & Sourdough · 베이커리

The sourdough and bagel movement that took over Jeju

Jeju's bakery scene has exploded — artisan sourdough, French croissants in converted stone barns, and bagel museums with ocean views. The island's volcanic wheat culture has met the third-wave fermentation movement. These are the spots worth a 빵지순례 (bread pilgrimage).

🥯
Viral

런던베이글뮤지엄 제주London Bagel Museum — Jeju

₩3,000–6,000
Handmade Bagels & Cream Cheese · 수제 베이글

The SNS sensation. Handmade bagels with a constantly rotating cast of cream cheeses — pecan is the fan favourite. The Jeju branch sits on the east coast road with a full-wall glass dining room overlooking the ocean. Wait is mandatory — takeaway moves faster. Near Cafe Layered and Blue Elephant Space.

Why the queue: The bagels are genuinely good — chewy, slightly sweet, and the cream cheeses are made in-house. But it's the combination of bagel + ocean view that makes the photos circulate. The museum sells branded mugs and tumblers too — the kind of merch that signals you were here.
제주시 구좌읍 동복로 85 · East coast road 08:00–18:00 · Paid parking · Remote queuing available
🥯
Healthy

블루메베이글Blume Bagel

₩2,500–5,000
Health-Focused Bagels · 건강 베이글

The quiet alternative to the London Bagel Museum. No egg, no butter, no oil (except in a few specialty items). Cream cheese made from Jeju milk, boiled in-house. The 소금베이글 (salt bagel) and 베이컨콘치즈베이글 are signatures. Near the airport — perfect first or last meal. Pet-friendly.

제주시 가령로4길 3 · Near Jeju Airport 09:00–18:00 daily · Free parking · Pet-friendly
🥐
#1 2025

우드노트 베이커리Woodnote Bakery

₩3,000–7,000
French Sourdough & Croissants · 프랑스식 천연발효

Voted #1 Jeju bakery of 2025. French-style croissants, baguettes, and 깜빠뉴 (campagne) made with Jeju ingredients: matcha croissant, tangerine campagne, natural fermentation. The space is a converted Jeju stone barn (돌창고) — an SNS photo sanctuary as much as a bakery.

The vibe: This is what happens when a serious French-trained baker meets Jeju's volcanic stone architecture. The croissants shatter properly. The campagne has the open crumb you only get from long, cold fermentation. Come early — they sell out of the croissants by early afternoon.
서귀포시 안덕면 화순리 769 · Near Sanbangsan 10:00–18:00 · Closed Wednesdays
🍞
Sourdough

백마베이크Baekma Bake

₩3,000–6,000
48-Hour Sourdough · 천연발효종 사워도우

The purist's sourdough. A Jeju resident who missed the sourdough from their time in Australia tracked this place down — and it became a destination. 48-hour cold fermentation with natural starter. Minimal ingredients. "Bread that sits comfortably in your stomach" (속이 편한 빵).

제주시 시내 · Jeju City centre Search Naver for current hours
🍊
Experience

웃뜨리Utturi

₩2,500–6,000
Fermentation Bakery + Tangerine Picking · 쉰다리 발효종

~50 bakery items and ~20 drinks, all made with 쉰다리 (a Jeju traditional fermented drink) as the starter. Highlights: 애월샌드 (Aewol sandwich), 르뱅쿠키, 한라봉무스. Set on a 1,000-pyeong tangerine farm with free tangerine picking. Three floors of antique furniture + outdoor terrace.

The experience: Buy a sandwich, pick free tangerines, eat on the terrace. This is the only bakery in Korea where the bread starter is made from a traditional Jeju drink. Pet-friendly, family-friendly, solo-friendly.
제주시 애월읍 광성로 213 · Aewol 09:00–18:00 daily · Free tangerine picking Pet-friendly · 3 floors

Institution

아베베 베이커리Abebe Bakery

₩2,000–5,000
Doughnuts, Baguettes & Castella · 도넛 · 베이커리

The Dongmun Market institution — 8,945 blog reviews and counting. Famous for 우도땅콩크림도너츠 (Udo peanut cream doughnuts), 앙버터 바게트, and milk bread. Three floors inside Dongmun Market Gate 12. The first bakery stop on many Jeju trips.

제주시 동문로6길 4 · Dongmun Market, Gate 12 (floors 1–3) 8,945 blog reviews · Also at Conan Beach (비킬라)

빵귿Bbang-Gyul

₩2,000–5,000
Tangerine Bakery · 감귤 베이커리

Tangerine croissant, tangerine castella, black pork sausage bread, salt bread. Requires open-run (lining up before opening) to get the full selection. Instagram-aesthetic interior. The tangerine croissant is the reason — citrus butter folded into proper laminate.

서귀포시 남원읍 태위로 152 · Namwon, south coast 09:00–17:00 · Closed Mon & Tue · Arrive before opening

Hard-to-Book & Reservation-Only · 예약 필수

The spots that require planning, DMs, or sheer luck

These are the places you can't just walk into — and that's why they matter. From a Netflix-star chef's sold-out Jeju debut to a secret glass building in the forest that isn't on any map. Book before you fly.

⭐
Netflix

이모카세 제주Emocase Jeju

₩15,000–27,000
Korean Fine Dining · 바다술상 콘셉트

The hottest opening of 2025. Chef Kim Mi-ryeong from Netflix's "흑백요리사" (Black & White Chef) chose Jeju for her first island restaurant. The concept is 바다술상 — "ocean drinking table" — Korean food designed to pair with drinks, by the sea. A 정식 set is just ₩15,000; 갈치조림 ₩27,000. On opening day (Sep 4, 2025), it sold out in 2 hours.

Why it's here: This is the convergence of Korean fine dining, Netflix fame, and Jeju's ingredient culture. The prices are shockingly fair for the pedigree — the chef's mainland reputation could command 3× more. Expect this to get harder to book as word spreads. Reserve via Naver the moment you book your flight.
제주시 한림해안로 582 · Hallim, by the sea 11:00–22:00 · Opened Sep 4, 2025 Reservation essential — sells out daily
🌲
Secret

엘엠엔티LMNT

Course pricing varies
Seasonal Course Dining · 시즌 한정 코스

The secret that isn't on any map. A glass building hidden in the forest near Jungmun, with views of both trees and ocean. Seasonal course menus only — 1 drink per person required. Reservations via Instagram DM only. Tuesday dinners are not available. If you can find it and get in, you've earned a story.

The legend: This is the kind of place people hear about from a friend who heard it from a friend. The glass building in the forest is the draw as much as the food — dining inside feels suspended between the canopy and the sea. The fact that it's DM-only booking keeps the mystique alive.
제주 중문 · Jungmun area (exact location via DM) Instagram DM reservation only · No Tuesday dinner
🍝
Italian

더 스푼The Spoon

₩18,000–35,000
Jeju Seafood Italian · 제주 해산물 이탈리안

Refined Italian built on Jeju seafood: 성게 파스타 (sea urchin pasta), 당근 퓨레 (carrot purée), 돌문어 샤프란 소스 옥돔 (octopus & saffron tilefish). High completion rate on every dish — locals and travellers both认可. Solo bar seating available. Book via Catchtable.

제주시 · Jeju City Catchtable booking · Solo bar available
🧀
Raclette

치저스Cheeses

₩15,000–25,000
Raclette Steak · 라클렌 스테이크

A food-truck success story turned brick-and-mortar. The signature is raclette steak — a cheese waterfall of melted Gouda poured over medium-grilled beef. The visual is the sell, but the taste backs it up. Reservations open every Tuesday at 19:00 on Naver — and fill instantly. Closed Tue–Thu.

Booking strategy: Set an alarm for Tuesday 7 PM. Cancellations get posted on their Instagram — check there if you miss the window. The cheese waterfall is one of those dishes that's better than its Instagram because the smell of melting raclette hits you before the plate lands.
제주 조천·구좌 · Northeast coast Naver booking opens Tue 19:00 · Closed Tue–Thu
🍣
Omakase

서문 수산Seomun Sushan

Course pricing varies
Aged Fish Omakase · 숙성회 오마카세

Jeju's whispered-about omakase. Aged fish (숙성회) served course-style: 보말죽 (sea snail porridge), 볶음우동, 딱새우장 (fermented shrimp), then wasabi rice for hand-built nigiri. Reservation requires a phone call + deposit — old-school and deliberate.

제주시 · Jeju City Phone reservation + deposit required

Summer & Beach · 여름 해변

Where the sand meets the table — Jeju's seasonal food culture

Jeju in summer is a beach-hopping, bakery-grazing, ocean-gazing circuit. These are the food spots positioned right on the sand — the ones that turn a beach day into a food day, or vice versa.

🏝
Beach

짐바란 비치Jimbalan Beach

₩5,000–9,000
Beachfront Bakery & Cafe · 발리 스타일 해변 카페

Bali-inspired outdoor tables right on the sand. Beach wind, bare feet, summer vibes. The bakery is pilgrimage-grade — the 짐바란선셋 signature drink is the move. This is the closest thing Jeju has to a Southeast Asian beach club.

Summer play: Arrive mid-afternoon, claim a beach table, swim between courses, stay for sunset. The sand is literally under your table. Free parking. This is what Jeju summer looks like when you stop chasing waterfalls and just sit by the sea.
제주시 애월읍 애월북서길 56 · Aewol, on the beach 09:00–21:00 daily · Free parking
🏖
Hanok

해지개Haejigae

₩5,000–8,000
Hanok Concept Beach Cafe · 한옥 카페

A traditional Hanok concept cafe facing Handam Beach (한담해변) in Aewol. The juxtaposition is the draw — Joseon-era architecture, modern bakery, ocean through the lattice windows. Signature drinks + a full bakery menu. Part of the Aewol cafe strip's southern expansion beyond the original Bomnal zone.

제주시 애월읍 애월북서길 52 · Handam Beach, Aewol Aewol cafe strip · beachfront
🌋
3-in-1 View

원앤온리One & Only

₩8,000–18,000
Brunch Cafe · 제주 풍광 압축

The view that "compresses Jeju into one spot": Sanbangsan mountain behind you, Hwangwooji Beach in front, the Brother Islands (형제섬) on the horizon. Brunch menu — the OAO Burger and the Sanbangsan Cake are signatures. Pet-friendly.

Why this view: Stand here and you understand why people build on Jeju. Mountain, ocean, islands — all three in one frame. This is the photo you'll send to someone to explain why Jeju is different. Come for golden hour.
서귀포시 안덕면 산방로 141 · Near Sanbangsan 09:00–19:00 daily · Free parking · Pet-friendly
🍩
Beach

아베베 베이커리 비킬라Abebe Bakery Bikilla

₩2,000–5,000
Beachfront Doughnut Pastry · 코난비치 도넛

The Abebe Bakery you already know from Dongmun Market, but this one is on Conan Beach (코난비치) — the east coast's quietest white-sand stretch. Doughnut pastries + beach = the ultimate summer morning. Walk in, grab a peanut cream doughnut, walk out onto the sand.

제주시 구좌읍 해맞이해안로 617-4 · Conan Beach, east coast Summer essential · beach + doughnuts
🥖
Beach

오드랑베이커리 함덕점Odrang Bakery Hamdeok

₩2,000–5,000
Beachfront Bakery · 마늘바게트 · 인절미브레드

A 3-minute walk from Hamdeok Beach — the east coast's most popular swimming and kayak-rental spot. Garlic baguette and injeolmi bread are the signatures. 4,371 blog reviews. The essential summer combo: swim at Hamdeok, dry off, eat bread, repeat.

제주시 조천읉 조함해안로 552-3 · 3 min walk from Hamdeok Beach 4,371 blog reviews · summer bread pilgrimage

Unique Finds · 특별한 발견

The dishes and spots that don't fit any category — and that's the point
🍜
Unique

한라면옥Hallamyeonok

₩8,000–10,000
Hallasan Noodles · 한라산 국수

A Jeju-only dish: bibim (spicy mixed) noodles topped with a piece of broth ice shaped like Mt. Hallasan. As the ice melts, it transforms the spicy bibim into a complex hybrid broth. Gimmicky? Yes. But it works — the temperature and dilution arc is genuinely interesting.

Also at 명랑식당 (Myeongrang Sikdang): The same dish, slightly different execution. Try both if you're a noodle completist. The "mountain" melting is genuinely photogenic — this is one viral food moment that earns its posts.
제주시 · Jeju City area Also at: 명랑식당
🥟
Authentic

천일만두Cheonil Mandu

₩9,000–25,000
Authentic Chinese Dumplings · 중국식 만두 전문

Authentic mainland Chinese dumplings in the middle of Seogwipo. Run by Chinese owners and staff. The signature 눈꽃만두 (snowflake dumplings, ₩10,000) — fried dumplings coated with starch water, crispy on top and juicy on bottom. The 부추군만두 (chive dumplings with shrimp) has "explosive juice." Don't skip the 마파두부 (mapo tofu, ₩12,000) — authentic Sichuan, numbing and spicy.

Why it's here: This is the kind of place you don't expect on Jeju — a genuinely good Chinese restaurant in a Korean food paradise. Tables fill up fast after break time. ~10 tables, open kitchen, cozy. Near Seogwipo 매일올레시장 (Olle Market).
서귀포시 서문로 25 · Near Olle Market, Seogwipo 10:00–22:00 · Break 14:00–17:00 · Closed Thursdays Phone: 0507-1414-9799 · No private parking — use 공영주차장
🍗
TV

희신이네Heuisinne

₩9,000–15,000
Korean-Chinese Chicken & Chinese Food · 닭요리 + 중식

Featured on YouTube's "또간집" (Tteoggan Jip) hosted by 풍자 (Poongja). A Korean-Chinese chicken and Chinese food specialist. The 닭고기냉밀면 (cold chicken noodle, ~₩9,000) is the blogger's #1 pick — a unique cold milmyeon with rich chicken broth. The 순살 닭깐풍기 (boneless fried chicken kanpunggi) is the other reason to come.

The name: Named after the owner's daughter. "Better when you lower your expectations," wrote one reviewer — then proceeded to eat everything. Open kitchen, long-established local feel. The chicken kanpunggi's sweetness may vary by who cooks it — that's part of the charm.
서귀포시 서홍로 175 · Seogwipo ~11:00–20:30 Also serves: 짬뽕, 해물짬뽕밥, 내장볶음밥
🏪
Market

동문시장Dongmun Market

₩2,000–15,000
Traditional Market Street Food · 시장 먹거리

Jeju's biggest traditional market. The place to eat 해물파전 (seafood & green onion pancake), sample fresh tangerines, find 빙떡 (buckwheat rice cake), buy 자리돔젓 (rabbitfish jeotgal), and graze through street food stalls. The energy is the attraction as much as the food.

Strategy: Come hungry, don't commit to one stall. Walk the lanes, taste as you go, buy tangerine gifts at the end. This is also where you'll find the best 오메기떡 (omegi rice cake) and Jeju-specific condiments to take home.
제주시 · Central Jeju City Open daily · best in morning for fresh produce, evening for food stalls
