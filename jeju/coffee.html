제주 커피 — 섬에서 굽는 빈

Jeju has quietly become one of Asia's deepest specialty coffee destinations. A Sprudge #1 global roaster. The world's only oak-firewood roasting operation. An all-you-can-drink espresso bar. A musician's hi-fi roastery you book before you fly. These aren't cafes with coffee — they're coffee destinations that happen to be on a volcanic island.

Something remarkable happened on Jeju over the last five years. The island's combination of volcanic water, a slow-living culture imported by Seoul escapees, and a critical mass of baristas who trained abroad produced a roasting scene that punches far above its weight.

Korean coffee columnist Sim Jae-beom said of Longplay: "a place worth traveling to Jeju just for the coffee." The Financial Times ranked Jeju's Momos Roastery among the 100 best coffee shops in the world. And Coffee Factory Gwideok roasts over oak firewood — a method so rare it may be the only one on the planet.

This guide covers 13 roasteries and specialty coffee bars across the island, from the old downtown of Jeju City to the east coast villages and down to Seogwipo. Each has its own philosophy: light-roast purists, espresso obsessives, brewing-only contrarians, firewood madmen. All rated and mapped.

The Jeju Coffee Cheat Sheet

  • Best for:World ranking → Momos Roastery (#22 FT)
  • Most unique:Oak firewood → Coffee Factory Gwideok
  • Best deal:All-you-can-drink → Pac.Par (₩6,000 till 11 AM)
  • Celebrity:Lee Sang-soon → Longplay (book ahead)

  • Quickest:Takeout window → Jeremy (Aewol)

Hi-Fi

롱플레이Longplay

₩5,000–8,000
Specialty Coffee + Hi-Fi Audio · 싱글오리진 · 블렌딩

Opened by musician Lee Sang-soon (이상순) — husband of Lee Hyori, Jeju's most famous transplant. Curated seasonal playlists on high-fidelity audio. Collaborates with Fritz, Rowkey, Center Coffee, and Montage for beans. Coffee columnist Sim Jae-beom called it "a place worth traveling to Jeju just for the coffee."

The play: This is what happens when a serious music obsessive applies the same curatorial discipline to coffee. The beans rotate seasonally, the playlists match the weather, and the room sounds as good as it tastes. Pre-reservation required — it's small and the reputation precedes it.
제주시 구좌읍 동복로 44 · Between Gujwa & Hamdeok, east coast Pre-reservation required · IG: @longplay_jeju Collabs: Fritz, Rowkey, Center Coffee, Montage

Fire

커피공장 귀덕Coffee Factory Gwideok

₩6,500
Oak-Firewood Direct-Fire Roasting · 참나무 장작 직화

The world's only oak-firewood direct-fire coffee roasting operation. Oak logs, real flame, direct contact — an impossibly rare method that produces a smoky depth no electric or gas roaster can replicate. Single-origin drip (Ethiopia, Guatemala, Yemen Matari, Kenya, Blue Mountain) for just ₩6,500. 시향 (bean nose tasting) before you order.

Why it matters: This isn't a gimmick — it's an act of madness that produces coffee you literally cannot get anywhere else on the planet. The firewood flavour is subtle, not overpowering — it reads as an extra layer of warmth beneath the origin character. Near Gwideok Beach on the west coast. Buy beans to take home — turnover is high so freshness is guaranteed. Also sells online via Naver Smart Store.
제주시 한림읍 일주서로 5896 · Near Gwideok, west coast Drip ₩6,500 · Bean selection: Ethiopia, Guatemala, Yemen, Kenya, Blue Mountain Also online via Naver Smart Store

Buffet

팩토리얼파크Pac.Par (Factorial Park)

Buffet ₩6,000 · Drinks ₩4,000+
Espresso Bar · La Marzocco · 올유캔드링크

Called "Jeju's best espresso coffee bar." A vintage La Marzocco with a unique group head produces extractions that are "chewy and punchy." The deal: all-you-can-drink coffee buffet until 11 AM daily — pour as many cups as you want from the day's rotating selection for ₩6,000. Signatures: 서핑 플레이 (espresso + muscat lemon tea slush), 레몬로마노.

The buffet: This is the most generous coffee offer in Korea. Arrive before 11, pay ₩6,000, taste everything. The owners — a former Jeju local and a Jeju native — built this for the love of extraction. The vintage La Marzocco is the visual anchor; sit at the bar and watch them work.
제주시 남성로 154-2 1층 · Old downtown Jeju City Coffee buffet until 11:00 AM daily · IG: @pac.par

Global

모모스 로스터리Momos Roastery & Coffee Bar

₩5,000–8,000
Globally Acclaimed Roaster · 글로벌 스페셜티

The globally recognised name. Named #1 "Notable Roaster" by Sprudge (2022) — the coffee world's most influential publication — and ranked #22 in the Financial Times' "100 Best Coffee Shops in the World" (2026). Direct-trade single origin. Long coffee bar where you watch roasting and brewing happen in real time. Drip bags and green beans to go.

서귀포시 · Seogwipo Sprudge #1 Notable Roaster 2022 · FT Top 100 World 2026

Light

커피라이트 로스터스Coffee Light Roasters

₩4,500–7,000
Light-Roast Pour-Over Specialist · 라이트 로스팅

The name means both "coffee leads the way" and a commitment to light roasting. The most impressive roasting specialty shop in Jeju's old downtown. A professional coffee columnist wrote: "the Peru Geisha was the best I had all year." Rotating seasonal beans — anaerobic Costa Rica, Peru Geisha. Natural, uncontrived atmosphere with genuine hospitality.

제주시 서사로 10-2 · Old downtown Jeju City Pour-over focus · IG: @coffeelight_roasters
🧊
Cold

무우수 커피 로스터스Muusu Coffee Roasters

₩4,500–7,000
Bright Roast + Cold Brew Einspänder · 콜드브루 아인슈페너

"Muusu" means the Tree of No Worries (보리수나무). Beloved by locals and travellers alike since opening. Four rotating single-origin beans for brewing; the signature 콜드브루 아인슈페너 (cold brew with vanilla cream) is one of the best on the island. Freshly baked canelés daily. Second location 무우수 홀리데이 at Hwabuk Harbor offers ocean views.

The affogato move: Their cold brew einspänder — cold brew topped with vanilla cream — is the most-ordered drink and the most photographed. The cream slowly sinks into the coffee, creating a gradient. Daily canelés pair perfectly.
제주시 조천읍 조천11길 22-2 · Jocheon Also: 무우수 홀리데이 at 화북포구 (ocean view) Daily canelés · 4 rotating beans · decaf available

Ocean

바이러닉 에스프레소 바Byronic Espresso Bar — Jeju

₩4,500–7,000
Espresso Bar + Pudding · 에스프레소 + 푸딩

The signature Jeju location of Seoul's popular espresso bar chain. A three-floor black-and-white minimalist space near Iho Tewoo Beach, 8 minutes from the airport. Focused on "just two things: chewy pudding and rich espresso." The Byronic Pudding (whiskey or caramel) has a cult following separate from the coffee. Sells roasted beans and branded goods.

The architecture: This building is a statement — black-and-white geometric, ocean-facing, three floors of seating. Order on ground, sit upstairs with the view. 8 minutes from the airport makes this the perfect first or last stop on the island.
제주시 테우해안로 96 · Near Iho Tewoo Beach, ~8 min from airport 3-floor space · ocean view · roasted beans sold on-site

Brew

그린루스카Green Ruska

₩5,000–7,000
Brewing-Only Specialty Coffee · 브루잉 전문 (에스프레소 없음)

The only brewing-only specialty coffee shop in Jeju's old downtown — no espresso machine, just pour-over. Run by a former Haso Local Coffee and Café Imagei barista. Housed in a remodeled traditional Jeju stone-wall house (구축). The signature 카페페퍼밀크 (oat milk + organic black pepper from Azad Hind farm) sounds insane and works beautifully.

제주시 서사로12길 45 1층 · Old downtown, Samdo-dong Brewing only — no espresso · Stone-wall house · IG: @green_ruska
🌊
Tea

하토우 커피 로스터스Hatou Coffee Roasters

₩7,000–8,000
Light Roast + Tea Ceremony Service · 환차 서비스

"Hatou" means "wave" in Japanese — the owner discovered specialty coffee during a year living in Japan. Light-roast specialist. Each cup comes with detailed tasting notes (origin, processing method, flavour profile) and a welcome tea before the drip — a tea-ceremony sensibility applied to coffee. Colombia Granja Paraíso 92 Vanilla, Ethiopia Haru Suke Kurume.

Why it's a hidden gem: This is a relatively unknown spot in Hado-ri, Gujwa — small, intimate, focused purely on coffee excellence. The welcome-tea-before-drip ritual is unique on Jeju. If you're the type who reads origin labels, this is your spot.
제주시 구좌읍 구좌로 188-1 · Hado-ri, Gujwa Welcome tea service · tasting notes per cup · decaf available

To-Go

제레미Jeremy

₩4,000–5,000
Takeout-Only Roastery Stand · 테이크아웃 로스터리

A tiny takeout-only roastery stand on the Aewol cafe street. Minimalist and focused purely on quality coffee to-go. No seating, no view, no food — just freshly roasted beans, pulled into a cup, handed through a window. The anti-thesis of the ocean-view cafe culture Aewol is known for. Coffee for people who care about coffee.

제주시 애월읍 애월로 106-1 · Aewol cafe street Takeout only · no seating · in-house roasting

KTO

구씨커피로스터스Gu's Coffee Roasters

₩4,500–6,000
Self-Roasted Village Drip · 셀프로스팅 드립

A Korea Tourism Organisation-listed village roastery in Sehwa-ri, opened 2019. Calm, nuanced single-origin drip coffee from self-roasted beans — each bean's unique flavour expressed through diverse extraction methods. Known locally as a "coffee artisan" shop. The kind of place where the roaster talks you through the bean before brewing.

제주시 구좌읍 구좌로 21 · Sehwa-ri village, Gujwa KTO-listed · since 2019 · diverse extraction methods

Judge

나인브로스커피Nine Bros Coffee

₩4,000–6,000
Roasting + Barista Training · 로스팅 + 바리스타 교육

Run by two brothers with serious credentials — the older brother is an international barista competition judge, and the younger is an active competitor. Three bean choices: easy-drinking, aromatic, or acidic. Also runs barista training, café consulting, and wholesale bean supply. VisitJeju-recognised for coffee passion.

서귀포시 대정읍 중산간서로 2595 · Southwest Jeju, near English Education City 3 bean choices · barista training available

Tropical

유동커피Yudong Coffee

₩4,000–5,500
Seasonal Single Origin · 시즌 싱글오리진

Started as a Jeju local roastery that has now expanded to 20+ locations nationwide. Seasonal blends with remarkably vivid tropical fruit aromas — so aromatic they're sometimes mistaken for flavoured coffee. A testament to Jeju's growing influence on Korea's national coffee culture. The Seogwipo original is still the best.

서귀포시 태평로 406-1 · Seogwipo Seasonal single origin · 20+ national locations
