West Coast · 애월 · 한림 · 한경

Black pork country, ocean-view grills, and fusion haenyeo pasta

The drive from Jeju City northwest through Aewol to Hallim and Hyeopjae is the island's most photographed coastline — and its richest black-pork corridor. This is where the 근고기 (thick-cut) style was born.

★
Gem

돈사촌Donsachon

₩15,000–30,000
Black Pork · 근고기 원조 · 연탄불

The originator (원조) of Jeju's 근고기 style — extra-thick-cut black pork neck and belly grilled over 연탄불 (charcoal briquette fire). The meat juices are, per the blogger who made it their #1 pick, "truly fantastic" (육즙이 환상적). Operates like a butcher shop: fresh meat at fair prices, not a fancy restaurant. This is the style locals prefer over tourist-oriented grills.

Dip like a local: 멜젓 (멸치젓, anchovy sauce) — the only correct dip for 근고기. The briquette charcoal gives a deeper, smokier char than standard charcoal. This combination is why locals drive across the island to get here.
제주시 애월 · Aewol area Butcher-shop style · Cash & card Ask for: 근고기 (thick-cut neck & belly) + 멜젓

Local

고향흑돼지Gohyang Heukdwaeji

₩44,000 / 2-person set
Black Pork BBQ with Ocean View · 흑돼지 세트

Recommended by a local accommodation host near Hyeopjae Beach. Features an ocean view through large windows while you eat. The 2-person set (₩44,000) includes 180g belly + 180g neck + 2 abalone + 4 shrimp + sausage. The owner grills everything for you. Pork neck is "tender like beef."

Order this: Add 고사리 (bracken fern) to the grill — it chars alongside the pork fat and absorbs the drippings. The black pork + bracken combo is a Jeju institution. Strongly recommended if you're in the Hallim or Hyeopjae area.
제주시 한림읍 한림해안로 29 · Near Hyeopjae Beach 11:00–22:00 · Closed Wednesdays · Phone: 064-796-6426 Large parking · Owner grills for you

Gem

밥깡패Bapgangpae

₩12,000–18,000
Jeju Fusion · 해녀파스타 · 흑돼지 두부 커리

Long waits but worth it. Creative fusion built entirely from local Jeju ingredients: 해녀파스타 (haenyeo pasta — seafood pasta using what the female divers catch that day) and 흑돼지 두부 커리 (black pork tofu curry). Closes when ingredients run out — arrive at opening time.

The name: "밥깡패" literally means "rice gangster" — someone who dominates the meal so thoroughly that rice is all that's left to talk about. The kind of self-aware naming that signals a confident kitchen.
제주시 한림 · Hallim area Closes when sold out · Arrive at opening Limited parking

블랙씨걸Black Sea Girl

₩40,000 (medium)
Mackerel Sashimi & Stew · 고등어회 · 지리탕

꽁순이네Kongsun-i-ne

₩8,000
Hidden Gogi-Guksu · 고기국수

A true hidden gem in the quiet Aewol mid-mountain (중산간) area. Surrounded by lush greenery with a spacious parking lot — relaxed and away from the coastal crowds. Clean interior and presentation. East Coast · 성산 · 구좌 · 월정리

Haenyeo divers, Sunrise Peak noodles, and the freshest conch ramen on earth

The east is wilder, less developed, and home to Jeju's haenyeo (female free-diver) culture. The food here is pulled from the ocean that morning — literally.

★
Gem

해녀의 집Haenyeo House

₩10,000–20,000
Haenyeo Seafood · 해물라면 · 성게 보말죽

The author's #1 pick for "the taste of Jeju's sea." More popular with neighbourhood residents than tourists — a sign of authenticity. Seafood is freshly caught by female divers (해녀) that morning. The 해물라면 (seafood ramen) — made with freshly caught conch (소라) and abalone (전복) — has broth that is "on another level." Also try 성게 보말죽 (sea urchin & abalone porridge).

Call ahead: Closes early when ingredients run out — which happens often, because a haenyeo's daily catch is unpredictable. Call to confirm they're open before driving out. This is not a place you can plan around; it plans itself around the tide.
제주시 구좌읍 · Near Woljeongri / Sehwari Closes when food runs out · Call ahead
p
Local

옛날옛적Yennalyennyeok

₩15,000–25,000
Black Pork · 흑돼지 주물럭

A warm, cozy black-pork restaurant in the tourist-heavy Seongsan area that is surprisingly recognised by locals — a rare find. Their 흑돼지 주물럭 (seasoned raw/minced black pork) is praised for seasoning that "doesn't overdo it — you taste the natural meat flavour." The name means "Once Upon a Time," and the atmosphere delivers.

Solo diner alert: Some black-pork restaurants require a 2-person minimum order. Check in advance if you're eating alone — Seongsan area spots are particularly strict about this.
제주시 성산 · Seongsan area Check minimum order for solo diners

가시아방 국수Gasiabang Guksu

₩8,000 · Set ₩32,000
Gogi-Guksu · 고기국수 · 커플세트

A long-established gogi-guksu restaurant near Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak). High turnover means shorter waits despite long lines. Supports online/app-based waiting. The 커플세트 (couple set, ₩32,000) is a must-try — includes noodles, domebegi pork, and sides. Extremely busy every January 1st for sunrise crowds.

Local

국수마을Guksu Maeul

₩7,000–10,000
Gogi-Guksu & Sea Urchin Noodles · 고기국수 · 성게칼국수

Called the "sacred place for Jeju taxi drivers" — the ultimate endorsement. A driver's restaurant with good taste and generous portions at ₩7,000, lower than the famous spots nearby. Serving for 20 years with a loyal regular clientele. The sleeper hit: 성게칼국수 (sea urchin knife-cut noodles, ₩10,000), made with sea urchin freshly caught by haenyeo divers.

Taxi driver wisdom: When in doubt in Jeju, ask a taxi driver where they eat. This place is why. The broth is "savory yet mild" — and at ₩7,000, it's the best-value bowl on the east coast.
제주 서귀포시 성산읍 동류암로 30 · Near Seongsan 20-year establishment

South Coast · 서귀포 · 중문

Pufferfish milt, rare mackerel sashimi, market noodles, and Jeju heritage food

Seogwipo's food scene runs deeper than its tourist reputation suggests. Inside the 올레시장 (Olle Market) and along the southern coast, you'll find Jeju's most unusual specialties: pufferfish stew with milt, mackerel sashimi so fresh it has no smell, and reimagined heritage dishes.

★
Gem

몰질 식육식당Moljil Sikyuk Sikdang

₩8,000–25,000
Pufferfish Stew & Meat Jjamppong · 복지리 · 고기짬뽕

One of the most distinctive restaurants on the island. The name 몰질 is Jeju dialect for "horse-riding path." Known locally as a 복지리 (pufferfish stew) specialist — the stew contains pufferfish and "애" (pufferfish milt/soft roe), "a symbol of freshness." Cooked at the table with water parsley, mushrooms, bean sprouts. Selected for the 제주 고메 FESTA.

Two things to know: (1) Pufferfish dishes sell out fast — visit before noon. The milt is "delicate and soft," the meat "firm and chewy." (2) The 고기짬뽕 (meat jjamppong, ₩8,000) is a sleeper hit — loaded with cabbage and pork, "clean, not greasy, moderately spicy." Two completely different dishes in one kitchen, both exceptional.
제주 서귀포시 이어도로 598 10:30–15:00 · Closed Sundays · Phone: 064-739-1542 Pufferfish sells out by 12:00 — arrive early

Gem

자연수산Jayeon Sushan

₩30,000–50,000
Mackerel Sashimi & Stew · 고등어회 · 지리탕

Run by an elderly couple. Fills up fast after 6 PM. Closes when ingredients run out — same-day phone call essential. The 고등어회 (fresh mackerel sashimi) is described as "not easily found even for Jeju residents." No fishy smell, extremely fresh, tender texture. Served with lavish sides: seaweed, pickled onions, rolled eggs, pan-fried dishes.

How to eat it: The owner's recommended method — mix rice with gim (seaweed) and sesame seeds, top with seaweed and mackerel sashimi, eat with pickled onions. "A best single bite combination." The meal ends with 지리탕 (mackerel bone stew with cabbage and cheongyang peppers) — "cuts through any richness."
제주 서귀포시 서호남로32번길 44 Daily 17:00–21:30 · Phone: 064-739-8588 Call same-day to confirm availability

고씨네 천지국수Go's Family Cheonji Noodles

₩7,000–15,000
Anchovy-Meat Broth Noodles · 천지국수

Inside Seogwipo's 올레시장 (Olle Market). The signature 천지국수 (₩9,000) uses a unique anchovy-meat broth — a combination found only in Jeju. Clean, deep, yet light. Thin wheat noodles with tender meat and a generous portion. Also serves 흑돼지만두 (black pork dumplings, ₩5,000), 비빔국수 (₩9,000), and 수육 (boiled pork, ₩15,000).

"Often closes early when ingredients run out" — even before the 15:30 closing time. 7–9 tables only; locals wait in line. Near Seogwipo old bus terminal, Cheonjiyeon Falls, and Saebyeol Bridge — a perfect market-and-noodles afternoon.
제주 서귀포시 중앙로79번길 4 · Inside Olle Market 09:30–15:30 · LO 15:00 · Closed Fridays Phone: 0507-1318-0436 · Parking: 중앙로터리 공영주차장

Gem

맛존디 본점Matjondi

₩9,000–10,000
Reimagined Jeju Heritage Food · 보말죽 · 고기국수

Takes traditional Jeju foods that locals have eaten for generations and refines them. The 보말죽 (sea snail porridge, ₩10,000) — 보말 is Jeju dialect for sea conch — has a pumpkin-like color, savory with a slightly bitter edge. The 고기국수 (₩9,000) uses a "milky, rich bone broth that's energizing." Side dishes are served by a robot — a unique IoT touch.

What is 보말? It's the Jeju word for 고둥 (sea conch/whelk) — called 보말 on the island, 표준어 (standard Korean) elsewhere. Eating 보말죽 here is like tasting a piece of Jeju linguistic and culinary heritage simultaneously. Pet-friendly.
제주 서귀포시 태평로 36 09:30–15:30 · LO 15:00 · Sundays open 10:00 Phone: 0507-1343-1278 · Large parking · Pet-friendly

중앙식당Jungang Sikdang

₩8,000–12,000
Pork Bone Soup · 접짝뼈국

A hidden gem near Olle Market with a true local atmosphere — "where Jeju elders enjoy morning drinks (반주)." The signature 접짝뼈국 is pork bone soup served in a 뚝배기 (earthenware bowl), with broth that is "deeply simmered, rich and savory" (진하고 구수하다). Solo-dining friendly. The author's top pick for "든든한 아침" (hearty breakfast).

제주 서귀포시 · Near Olle Market Morning hours · Kid-friendly menu

고집돌우럭Gojip Dolureok

₩15,000–30,000
Braised Rockfish & Filefish Sashimi · 우럭조림 · 자리돔회

Despite being near the Jungmun Tourist Complex, it's frequented more by Jeju locals than tourists — the key signal. Uses seasonal fish to prepare fresh dishes daily. 우럭 조림 (braised rockfish) and 자리돔 회 (raw filefish sashimi) are the signatures. Lunchtime has waiting lines — reservation recommended.

제주 서귀포시 중문동 · Near Jungmun Tourist Complex Reservation recommended for lunch

제미니국수 서귀포점Gemini Noodles — Seogwipo

₩32,000 / 2-person set
Gogi-Guksu with Farm-Direct Ingredients · 고기국수

A 1-minute walk from Sogeummak (쇠소깍). Uses fresh hand-pulled noodles (생면) and carefully simmered bone broth. Ingredients sourced directly from the owner's parents' farm. Serves 아강발 (a local pork offal dish) with makgeolli for older patrons, and mandu for kids. The Gemini A Set for 2 (₩32,000) is the way to go.

청송 고기국수Cheongsong Gogi-guksu

₩7,000
Best-Value Gogi-Guksu & Black Pork Suyuk · 고기국수 · 흑돼지 수육

A long-running traditional old restaurant (노포) in old Seogwipo downtown — the cheapest gogi-guksu on the island at ₩7,000.
